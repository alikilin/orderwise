Frequently asked

How do free scans work?
Your first 3 menu scans are free. OrderWise Pro ($9.99/year) unlocks 100 menu scans every month. It auto-renews yearly and you can cancel anytime in your App Store settings.

I subscribed but it shows locked.
Open Profile → Go Pro → Restore Purchase, signed in with the same Apple ID you subscribed with.

Are the allergen warnings reliable?
They are AI estimates based on typical recipes and can be wrong. Always confirm allergens and ingredients with the restaurant — especially for serious allergies.

What happens to my photos and profile?
Menu photos are read on your device; only the extracted menu text is sent for AI analysis. Your taste profile never leaves your device. No account, no ads, no tracking.
