Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

Overview

OrderWise ("the app", "we", "us") helps you decide what to order by analyzing menu photos. This policy explains what data the app handles and how. We built OrderWise to collect as little as possible.

Data Stored on Your Device

Your taste profile — dietary preferences, allergens, spice tolerance, favorite cuisines — is stored only on your device using Apple's local storage. We do not upload, sync, or back up this profile to our servers, because we do not operate servers that hold your personal data.

Menu Photos & Text

When you scan a menu, text is extracted from the photo on your device using Apple's Vision framework. The extracted menu text — together with your taste profile — is sent to our AI provider (OpenAI) solely to generate dish recommendations. The photo itself never leaves your device. Menu text is not stored after the recommendation is returned.

Third-Party AI Processing

Dish analysis is performed by OpenAI's API. The menu text and profile preferences you submit are processed under OpenAI's API data policies, which state that API inputs are not used to train their models. We send only the data needed to produce a recommendation and no information that directly identifies you.

Purchases

Your OrderWise Pro subscription is processed by Apple through StoreKit. We never see or store your payment details. Apple shares only whether your subscription is active, so the app can unlock features.

Analytics & Tracking

OrderWise does not include third-party advertising or tracking SDKs. We do not build advertising profiles and we do not sell your data — we have none to sell.

Children

OrderWise is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Your Choices

You can edit or reset your profile at any time from the Profile screen. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data from your device.
